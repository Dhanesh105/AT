About Andhra Treats

Bringing authentic Andhra flavors to your doorstep since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020 with a passion for preserving and sharing the rich culinary heritage of Andhra Pradesh, Andhra Treats began as a small family business with a big dream - to bring the authentic taste of traditional Andhra sweets and delicacies to food lovers everywhere.

Our journey started in the heart of Andhra Pradesh, where our founder learned the age-old recipes from their grandmother. These recipes, passed down through generations, form the foundation of every product we create today.

What sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to authenticity. We use only the finest ingredients, traditional cooking methods, and time-honored techniques to ensure that every bite transports you to the vibrant streets and homes of Andhra Pradesh.

What We Offer

Traditional Sweets

From the famous paper-thin Putharekulu to the rich and layered Kaja, our traditional sweets are made using authentic recipes and techniques passed down through generations.

Fruit Delicacies

Our Mamadi Thandra and other fruit-based treats offer a perfect balance of sweet and tangy flavors, made from the finest fruits and traditional preparation methods.

Authentic Pickles

Experience the bold and spicy flavors of Andhra with our traditional pickles, made with carefully selected spices and time-tested recipes.
